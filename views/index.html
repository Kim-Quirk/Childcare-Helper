About Childcare Helper


Our software was born out of a desire to streamline the daycare process. Shouldn't this be easier? Shouldn't there be a way to easily look up information? Keeping track of stacks of paper, charts, and remembering to tell parents anything important at the end of a long daycare day is hard. So we made it easier!


Our software digitizes the daycare process. Checking a child in is now a simple form that can be completed on any laptop, computer, or phone! Child misbehaves? There's a digital form for that too. Putting everything into a secure database allows for simple look-up, search functions, and simpler communication between the daycare and parents. All reports are compiled at the end of the day for when the child is picked up to ensure parents stay informed on what happened at daycare.
